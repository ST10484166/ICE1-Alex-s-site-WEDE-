My Story

Alex wasn’t great at small talk, but give him a keyboard and he could build entire worlds out of brackets and semicolons. While others made friends over lattes, Alex’s idea of mingling was debating whether `section` or `div` was more socially appropriate. His cat, Pixel, often served as the first beta tester, usually by walking across the keyboard. Despite being quiet in person, Alex’s websites spoke volumes: colorful, welcoming, and a little quirky. In Codeburg, Alex wasn’t just another coder, he was the introvert secretly holding the city together, one well-indented line at a time.
